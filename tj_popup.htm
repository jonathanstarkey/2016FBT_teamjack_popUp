Why Support Team Jack?

Pediatric brain cancer can affect a child’s ability to love, speak, see, run, and create. The leading cause of childhood cancer death, nearly 30% of all children diagnosed with brain cancer will pass, while many of the survivors will be left with a lifelong disability.

Pediatric cancer in general is largely underfunded (attaining a mere 4% of the National Cancer Institute’s funding), pediatric brain cancer is even moreso, with only 5% of that sum dedicated to research.
